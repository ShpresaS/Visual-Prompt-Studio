Assignment Title: Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.1

Prompt: Create a mural in spray paint on a subway wall, designed as a protest for feminism. The artwork should be in a busy subway where people can reflect on it. Its main message is to spread awareness for feminism and the necessity for women to feel safe in an enviroment with men. The subject matter in cludes women and girls of all diferent ages and races, broken chains, and flower going through concrete. The style should be abstract with pastels with bold messages to emphasize contast, symbolizing that not all women go through the same things and for women to feel safe no matter who they are.

Vocab: Medium- Different materials used to make art. For example, clay, spray paint and cloth.
Meaning- The ideas and reasoning behind an artwork.
Subject Matter- The things visible to us in an artwork. People, animals, and buildings are great examples.
Abstract Art- Where the subject matter is disoriented and blurred, but still able to be understood and recognized.
